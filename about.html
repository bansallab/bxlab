Location

The Bansal Lab is located on the 5th floor of Georgetown University's Science Center, Regents Hall. Shweta Bansal's office is 510, and the lab is in 561.

Directions + Parking

Regents Hall can be reached either through Entrance 1 (Reservoir Road NW and 38th St NW) or through the main campus entrance (37th St NW and O St NW)

If driving to campus, please use the Reservoir Road NW entrance, as there is ample metered parking available on Reservoir Road and the neighborbood to the North.

If taking the DC Metro, plase use the Red Line to arrive at Dupont Circle and then take the Georgetown GUTS shuttle to get to campus.
