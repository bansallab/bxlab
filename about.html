Location

The Bansal Lab is located on the 5th floor of Georgetown University's Science Center, Regents Hall. Shweta's office is 510, and the lab is in 561 with postdoc offices in 512.

Directions

Regents Hall can be reached either through Entrance 1 (3700 Reservoir Road NW, Washington DC) or through the Main Entrance (37th St NW and O St NW, Washington DC).

If driving to campus, please use Entrance 1, as there is ample metered parking available on Reservoir Road NW and the neighborbood to the North.

If taking the DC Metro, plase use the Red Line to arrive at Dupont Circle and then take the Georgetown Dupont GUTS shuttle to get to campus. Alternatively, you can take the Orange/Blue Line to arrive at Rosslyn and take the Rosslyn GUTS.
