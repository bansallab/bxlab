We want you.

At the Bansal Lab our work is inter-discplinary. We're always looking for:
  • biologists interested in applying/developing quantitative and computational methods to their systems
  • mathematicians and computational scientists interested in biology

Postdoctoral Opportunities

If you are interested in problems of disease ecology and public or animal health, we'd love to talk to you.
Qualifications
A doctorate in Applied Mathematics, Network Science, Biostatistics, Mathematical Biology, Theoretical Ecology, Epidemiology, or a closely related field.
How to Apply
Please email Shweta Bansal with a CV, a statement of research interest as they fit within the lab's research, and a list of references (letters not required at initial inquiry).
